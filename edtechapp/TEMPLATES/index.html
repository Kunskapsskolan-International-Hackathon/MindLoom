Building a More Equitable World through Education


G20 Initiative Workstream - ONE EARTH - ONE FAMILY - ONE FAMILY


The G20 Sherpa Tracks are working to build a more equitable world through education. They are committed to ensuring that all children and young people have access to quality education, regardless of their background. They are also working to make education more relevant to the needs of the 21st century economy. By investing in education, the G20 Sherpa Tracks are helping to create a more just and prosperous world for all.
